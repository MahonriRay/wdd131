Overview

Purpose

I want to create a website to explain the rules of Magic: The Gathering and how to play it because the game can be complex for newcomers, with its intricate mechanics and vast array of cards. A dedicated site would provide clear, accessible guides and tutorials to help new players understand and enjoy the game more quickly. Additionally, it could serve as a resource for existing players seeking to refine their skills and stay updated on the latest rules and strategies. Ultimately, the goal is to foster a welcoming and knowledgeable community for all Magic enthusiasts.

Audience

My target audience is those who are interested in Magic: The Gathering who have yet to play it, but want to.

Content

Home page

The homepage of my website will warmly welcome newcomers to the world of Magic: The Gathering, offering a user-friendly introduction to the game's fundamentals. Visitors can expect to find clear, concise guides on the basic rules, how to set up a game, and the essential mechanics. Additionally, the homepage will feature links to more detailed tutorials, and strategy tips. By providing a well-organized and informative entry point, the homepage will help new players feel confident and excited about diving into Magic: The Gathering.

History

The history subpage of my website aims to provide visitors with a comprehensive overview of Magic: The Gathering's rich and storied past. Here, you will discover the origins of the game, its evolution through various expansions, and significant milestones that have shaped its journey. This page is designed to give both newcomers and veteran players an appreciation for the game's legacy and its impact on the world of trading card games. Explore the history section to deepen your understanding of how Magic has grown into the beloved phenomenon it is today.

How To Play

The "How to Play" page is dedicated to guiding new players through the fundamentals of Magic: The Gathering. This section provides step-by-step instructions on setting up a game, understanding card types, and mastering the basic rules and gameplay mechanics. You'll find detailed explanations, illustrative examples, and helpful tips to make learning the game straightforward and enjoyable. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, this page will equip you with the knowledge you need to confidently play Magic: The Gathering.
